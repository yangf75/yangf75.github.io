  • Oct 26: Zhituo Wang (Harbin Institute of Technology)

    Title: Phase Transitions in Quantum Many-body system

    Abstract: In this talk I will present some recent progress on the construction of ground state of the honeycomb Hubbard model, which is a prototypical model for studying phase transitions in quantum many-body system. Using fermionic cluster expansions and constructive renormalization theory, we proved that the ground state of this model is not a Fermi liquid in the mathematical precise sense of Salmhofer. We also derived the non-perturbative critical temperature for the phase transition.

  • Oct 12: Pengfei Tang (Tianjin University)

    Title: Percolation on nonunimodular transitive graphs

    Abstract: Since the influential work of Benjamini and Schramm in 1996, percolation beyond the Z^d setting has received a lot of attention. In this talk, we will follow this trend and focus on the nonunimodular setting. The percolation models that will be discussed encompass Bernoulli percolation, uniform spanning forests and minimal spanning forests.
