  • Nov 2: Guohuan Zhao (Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science of CAS)

    Title: Stochastic equations with Divergence-free Drifts and its application

    Abstract: This talk focuses on time-inhomogeneous stochastic equations driven by additive noises with divergence-free drifts in Lorentz space $L^{\infty}(0,T;L^{d,\infty}(\mR^d))$. We will cover conditional and unconditional well-posedness, along with Aronson type estimates, followed by practical applications to the McKean-Vlasov equation with singular interaction kernels.
